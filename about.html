AI · Career

CVwise — AI CV Optimizer

Uploading your resume and pasting a job URL sounds simple — but the difference it makes is enormous. CVwise uses Claude AI to rewrite your CV sections to match the exact language, skills, and keywords of the job posting, dramatically improving your ATS score and the chances of a human actually reading your application. Whether you are a recent graduate or a seasoned professional, the tool adapts its output to your level and the role. Free plan includes three optimizations per month — more than enough to test whether this works for you.

Finance · Personal

BudgetWise — Personal Budget Tracker

Managing money sounds like it should be simple, yet most tools either require connecting your bank account or are too complicated to use daily. BudgetWise keeps things honest: you log your income, record your expenses, scan receipts with the OCR camera, and watch a clear monthly picture emerge. The year-end forecast tells you where you will stand in December based on your current habits. No bank connection, no subscriptions required to start — your financial data stays on your device unless you choose to sync it to your own account.

Documents · PDF

DocWise — Document Toolbox

Throughout a professional career, you encounter the same document problems repeatedly: a PDF that needs splitting before sending, a scanned invoice that must be made searchable, two versions of a contract that need to be compared side by side. DocWise handles all of it — merge, split, compress, OCR, image-to-PDF conversion, and document comparison — entirely inside your browser. No file is ever uploaded to a server. For anyone who handles sensitive documents, that privacy guarantee matters more than any cloud feature.

Data · Files

File Consolidator

Anyone who has worked with data knows the drill: twelve department reports in separate Excel files, all with the same column structure, that need to become one file before the meeting. File Consolidator does exactly that — drag in your CSV or Excel files, click merge, and download the combined result. It also merges PDFs and images into single documents. The entire operation runs locally in your browser. No file leaves your machine. No login. No size limits imposed by a server.
