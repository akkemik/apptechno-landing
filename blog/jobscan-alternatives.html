Understanding ATS: Why Optimisation Actually Matters

Applicant Tracking Systems are used by over 97% of Fortune 500 companies and the majority of mid-size employers. They don't just store applications — they rank them. Here's what modern ATS systems actually evaluate:

  • Keyword presence: Does your resume use the exact terminology from the job description? "Data visualisation" and "data viz" are not the same to a keyword parser.
  • Semantic relevance: Modern ATS platforms (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever) use NLP to understand context, not just word frequency.
  • Section structure: Standard headers (Experience, Education, Skills) parse correctly. Creative headers ("My Journey") confuse parsers.
  • File format: Word documents parse more reliably than PDFs in most ATS systems.
  • Dates and progression: Employment gaps and unexplained career changes are flagged in some systems.
Optimising for ATS is not about stuffing keywords — it's about speaking the same language as the job description. If they say "stakeholder management" and you say "working with clients," you may score lower even if the experience is identical.

The AI Advantage: Beyond Keyword Matching

Basic keyword checkers like JobScan or SkillSyncer tell you what is missing. AI tools like CVwise tell you how to add it naturally. The difference matters:

  • Basic tool: "Missing keyword: 'agile methodology'" — you add the phrase somewhere
  • AI tool: Rewrites your bullet point to demonstrate agile experience in context, making it both ATS-readable and compelling to human reviewers

The goal is not to pass ATS — it's to get a recruiter to call you. A resume that's optimised for machines but reads awkwardly to humans defeats the purpose. AI rewriting solves both problems at once.

FAQ

How much does JobScan cost?

JobScan costs $49.95/month for 50 scans or $89.95/month for unlimited. The annual plan is $149.95/year for 50 scans/month. There is a free plan limited to 5 scans/month. By comparison, CVwise offers AI-powered CV rewriting starting from free, with paid plans at $11.99/month.

Can I check my resume against ATS for free?

Yes. CVwise offers free ATS analysis and CV optimisation. Teal HQ's free plan includes keyword matching. SkillSyncer gives 5 free scans per month. For a full AI rewrite without limits, CVwise's paid plan at $11.99/month is the most affordable option.

Does ATS optimisation actually improve callback rates?

Yes, measurably. Studies by Resume Worded and Jobscan suggest that tailored resumes receive 2–3x more interviews than generic ones. The effect is strongest at large employers using automated screening and weakest at small companies where a human reads every application.

Is it ethical to use AI to optimise my resume?

Yes. AI tools help you present your genuine experience in the language recruiters and ATS systems understand. This is no different from working with a career coach or professional CV writer — you're communicating your existing skills more effectively, not fabricating experience you don't have.
