FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AppTechno products.

Are AppTechno products really free?

Yes, all AppTechno products are free to use at their core level. Some products like BudgetWise and CVwise offer optional premium plans for extended features such as higher usage limits, advanced AI capabilities, and cloud sync — but the free tier is generous and fully functional.

Do AppTechno products upload my files to a server?

No — never. All file processing for DocWise and File Consolidator happens entirely in your browser using WebAssembly and local JavaScript APIs. Your files never leave your device, and nothing is stored on any server. Products that require an account (BudgetWise, CVwise, BoatWise) only sync your own data to your personal cloud storage.

What is CVwise and how does the AI work?

CVwise is an AI-powered job application suite. You can build a professional CV from scratch using our guided 5-step builder, or upload your existing CV in PDF or DOCX format. Paste a job posting URL or description, and the Claude AI model (by Anthropic) rewrites your CV to match the role — improving ATS keyword alignment and generating a tailored cover letter. The free plan includes 3 optimizations per month.

What is BudgetWise?

BudgetWise is a free personal budget tracker available as a web app and Progressive Web App (installable on any device). It lets you track income and expenses by category, scan receipts with OCR, plan monthly budgets, and forecast your year-end financial position. It supports 6 languages and works across all devices with optional cloud sync.

What is DocWise and what file formats does it support?

DocWise is a free browser-based document toolbox with no uploads required. It supports PDF merging, splitting, and compression; OCR text extraction from scanned PDFs and images; image-to-PDF conversion (JPG, PNG, WebP); and side-by-side document comparison. All processing runs locally using WebAssembly, so your documents never leave your device.

What is BoatWise?

BoatWise is a complete boat and yacht management product for recreational boat owners. Track maintenance schedules and service history, manage your spare parts inventory, store equipment records and official documents, and keep a detailed voyage logbook. Available in 5 languages (EN, SV, DE, ES, TR), installable as a PWA on mobile. Free for the first 12 months.
