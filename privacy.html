AppTechno ("we", "our", or "us") operates the websites at apptechno.net and all of its subdomains and applications (including but not limited to BudgetWise, CVwise, DocWise, and any future apps published under the AppTechno platform). This Privacy Policy applies to all current and future services we operate and explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate our services:

  • Account data: Email address and name when you register for any AppTechno service that requires an account.

2. Local Processing

Most AppTechno tools (file consolidation, PDF merging, image conversion) process data entirely in your browser. No files are uploaded to our servers unless explicitly stated.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and Google AdSense cookies for advertising. See our Cookie Policy for details.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at info@apptechno.net.

7. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.
